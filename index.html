当前研究

Current Research

Animal-Specific 3D Reconstruction from a Single Image

Animal-Specific 3D Reconstruction from a Single Image

Nov. 2025 – Present

Computer Vision 3D Reconstruction Generative Models Multi-view Geometry 3DGS

这个项目试图解决一个具体但困难的问题：如何从单张动物图像出发，生成结构合理、视角一致的 3D 模型。 由于动物存在复杂姿态变化、非刚体形变，以及真实多视角数据稀缺的问题，我目前的思路是把视频生成、多视角伪监督和动物先验约束结合到同一条生成 pipeline 中。

This project targets a concrete but challenging question: how to generate a structurally plausible and view-consistent 3D model from a single animal image. Because animals involve complex pose changes, non-rigid deformation, and scarce real multi-view data, my current approach is to combine video generation, pseudo multi-view supervision, and animal-aware priors within one reconstruction pipeline.

问题定义

Problem Setting

单图到 3D 重建存在视角信息缺失；动物场景进一步带来非刚体形变和数据稀缺问题。

Single-image 3D reconstruction lacks viewpoint information, and animal data introduces non-rigid deformation and supervision scarcity.

方法设计

Method Design

通过视频生成模型合成伪多视角观测，再使用 AniMer 约束结构与姿态一致性，并接入改装后的 Hunyuan 生成框架。

The pipeline synthesizes pseudo multi-view observations with a video generation model, uses AniMer to constrain structure and pose consistency, and integrates both into a modified Hunyuan-based generator.

我负责的部分

My Role

负责整体 pipeline 搭建、模块衔接、训练与推理流程、结果可视化，以及基于失败案例的迭代优化。

I work on overall pipeline construction, module integration, training and inference workflow, result visualization, and iterative refinement based on failure-case analysis.

当前进展

Current Status

已经得到初步可行的 3D 生成结果，说明该 pipeline 具备 feasibility；当前仍在继续改进几何细节与稳定性。

The current prototype has produced a preliminary working 3D result, demonstrating feasibility; I am now continuing to improve geometric detail and robustness.
